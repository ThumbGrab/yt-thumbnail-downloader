How to Use YouTube Hashtags Effectively

Hashtags on YouTube help categorize your content and make it more discoverable. When viewers click a hashtag, they see a feed of other videos using the same hashtag. Used strategically, hashtags can help your video reach new audiences interested in your topic.

Where Hashtags Appear

When you add hashtags to your video description, the first three hashtags appear above your video title in a clickable blue format. This makes them highly visible and a valuable tool for categorization and discovery.

Hashtag Best Practices

  • Use 3-5 hashtags for the best results — quality over quantity
  • Place your most important hashtag first
  • Mix broad popular hashtags with specific niche ones
  • Only use hashtags that genuinely relate to your video
  • Avoid using more than 15 hashtags — YouTube may ignore them all

Important Hashtag Rules

YouTube has strict rules about hashtags. Do not use unrelated or misleading hashtags, as this violates YouTube's policies and can result in your video being removed. Also avoid overstuffing — if you add more than 15 hashtags, YouTube will ignore all of them.
